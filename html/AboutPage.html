beauty with vegan

VOrskins is a beauty e-commerce platform that provides local brands in the niche of vegan, cruelty-free, and non-toxic products. VOrskins believes that the benefits of natural skin care and body care will empower women in Indonesia. VOrskins also promotes in saving the environment by encouraging people to transition to natural products for daily uses.

Vision

To rise the fame of Indonesia’s diverse resources with vegan, cruelty-free, and toxic beauty produces

Mission

Conserve Indonesia's beauty production and gain and spreading awareness through partnered and potential brands.

History

Climate Change

The urge for climate action became crucial since 2020 was nomitnated as one of the three warmest year record, with an average temperature about 1.2 Celcius above the pre-industrial level.

Niche on Non-toxic and Vegan

Vegan skincare is one of the fastest-growing areas in the beauty industry. As more people opt for healthier, cleaner, and kinder products we can start to see the change we want to be in the world. By choosing vegan beauty products it is more likely to avoid toxins, unclean and unsafe ingredients found in non-vegan products.

Cruelty Free

Around 500,000 animals suffer and die just for cosmetics each year (The Humane Society International - Australia). Hence, this cruelty is also very wasteful and unnecessary. By realizing the impact of animal testing, VOrskins did a collective research to grab participants attention to cruelty-free products.

Partnerships

To support green movement, VOrskins decided to collaborate with Indonesia’s famous brands to support and enrich vegan beauty. We have merged partnerships with True to Skin, Mineral Botanica, Sensatia Botanicals, and Lacoco

Mini Earth Hero Program

2% of all purchases will be used as donations funs or climate action activities. Our passion for the Earth starts from our activities such as researching, collecting trash, replanting forest trees, and supporting children to urge in climate action.
